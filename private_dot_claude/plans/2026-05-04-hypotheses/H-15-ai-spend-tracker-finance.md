# H-15 — AI subscription spend tracker for finance/IT teams

**Category:** Aggregation gap + newly-public information

## Claim
Companies are bleeding on scattered AI subscriptions (ChatGPT, Claude, Cursor, Copilot, Midjourney, Notion AI, Otter, Granola, etc.) — many billed personally and expensed, many duplicated across teams. Finance/IT lacks visibility. A "AI spend audit" SaaS — CSV credit-card upload, identifies AI vendors, deduplicates, recommends consolidation — at $499 one-time or $99/mo SaaS converts.

## Why missed
- IT spend management tools (Zylo, Vendr) are enterprise-priced.
- The AI sub-vertical is new enough that tooling lags.
- Selling finance/IT B2B is structurally slow.

## Falsifier
Dies if: existing Vendr/Zylo / SaaS spend tools already cover this (must check their AI-vendor coverage); finance teams don't separate AI spend from general SaaS spend; CSV upload UX is too friction-heavy for self-serve.

## Confirmer
Mid-market finance/IT forums discussing AI sprawl; CFO surveys mention AI spend; existing tools are enterprise-priced not SMB.

## Payout
$499 one-time × 20–60 = $10–30k. Plausible to clear $10k. Or $99/mo × 100 = $9.9k.

## Cost-to-falsify
Medium. ~5 hours: Vendr/Zylo feature audit, CFO/IT survey scan, demand probe via Twitter/LinkedIn finance leaders.

## Bottom-line
**Plausible.** Single high-ticket enterprise sale clears the goal. Risk: enterprise sales cycle. Advance to Phase 2 mid priority.
