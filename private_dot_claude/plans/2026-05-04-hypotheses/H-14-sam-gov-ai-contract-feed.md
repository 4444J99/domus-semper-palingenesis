# H-14 — SAM.gov AI/ML federal contract opportunity feed

**Category:** Underexploited public data

## Claim
SAM.gov is the official federal contract opportunities portal. AI/ML startups want federal contracts but find SAM.gov's UX hostile. A weekly curated digest of "AI/ML/data science federal contract solicitations" at $49–99/mo converts to AI startup founders chasing govtech revenue.

## Why missed
- SAM.gov is free but UX is government-grade.
- BiQ / Govly / similar incumbents charge enterprise prices ($500+/mo).
- The startup-friendly mid-tier ($49–99/mo) doesn't have an obvious incumbent.

## Falsifier
Dies if: free SAM.gov filters are good enough; existing $49–99/mo competitor exists (HigherGov, Govly Lite — must check); audience size of AI/ML startups chasing federal contracts is too small.

## Confirmer
AI startup founder forums (Indie Hackers, YC alumni list) discussing federal contracts; high-end incumbents charge $500+ proving willingness-to-pay; SAM.gov API permits free-tier scraping.

## Payout
$49–99/mo × 50–200 subs = $2.5–20k/mo. Plausible to clear $10k.

## Cost-to-falsify
Medium. ~5 hours: SAM.gov API rate-limit research, competitor scan (HigherGov, GovWin, Govly), AI-startup-federal-contract demand audit.

## Bottom-line
**Plausible.** Demand exists; pricing gap is real. Advance to Phase 2 mid priority.
