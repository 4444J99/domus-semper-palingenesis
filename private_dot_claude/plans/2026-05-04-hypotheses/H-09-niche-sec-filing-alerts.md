# H-09 — Niche-industry SEC filing alert feed

**Category:** Newly-public information / underexploited data

## Claim
EDGAR is free but high-volume; investors and operators in niche industries (psychedelics, AI, lithium mining, defense tech, cannabis, biotech sub-segments) want vertical-specific real-time alerts on 10-K/10-Q/8-K filings, S-1 registrations, insider trades. A focused vertical alert feed at $19–49/mo is monetizable.

## Why missed
- Bloomberg / FactSet sell this at enterprise prices.
- Free EDGAR is too noisy to be useful out-of-the-box.
- Each vertical is too small for incumbents to bother with.

## Falsifier
Dies if: free Substack newsletters already cover the verticals (probable for AI, biotech); willingness-to-pay among retail/prosumer investors is below the $19/mo floor; SEC EDGAR rate limits prevent free-tier scaling.

## Confirmer
Active retail-investor Discord/Reddit communities in target verticals; ≥1 paid newsletter at >$10/mo with proven subs in adjacent verticals; EDGAR API permits the volume needed.

## Payout
$19–49/mo × 50–300 subs = $950–14700/mo. Plausible to clear $10k in 30 days if vertical has hot momentum and audience already aggregates.

## Cost-to-falsify
Medium. ~5 hours: pick 3 candidate verticals, scan paid newsletter offerings, count Reddit/Discord audience, EDGAR rate-limit math.

## Bottom-line
**Plausible.** Rev model is cleaner than most. Risk: each vertical's audience is small; need to nail the right vertical. Advance to Phase 2 mid-priority.
