# H-20 — FOIA-release real-time alert feed for journalists & researchers

**Category:** Newly-public information

## Claim
Federal FOIA releases drop continuously across agencies (FBI Vault, DoJ, DoD, etc.) with little aggregation. Journalists, researchers, and activists want real-time alerts on releases matching keywords/agencies. A subscription at $19–49/mo for "FOIA release alerts in your beat" converts.

## Why missed
- MuckRock and DocumentCloud cover the request side, not real-time release alerts cleanly.
- Government release pages have no unified RSS or API.
- Buyers (journalists, indie researchers) are often broke.

## Falsifier
Dies if: MuckRock already provides this; willingness-to-pay among journalists is too low; release surface is too thin to justify alerts; legal/ToS issues with scraping government release pages.

## Confirmer
Journalist/researcher Twitter discussing missed FOIA releases; existing newsletters cover similar surface; comparable academic-research feeds prove $19–49/mo tier.

## Payout
$19–49/mo × 50–300 subs = $950–14k/mo. Plausible to clear $10k if niche resonates.

## Cost-to-falsify
Medium. ~5 hours: MuckRock feature audit, journalism Twitter probe, scrape feasibility test on FBI Vault et al.

## Bottom-line
**Plausible niche play.** Audience is small but passionate. Advance to Phase 2 lower priority.
