**Layer orphaning:** A layer is spec'd but never built. The audit found all 7 cascade layers are "spec-only" — no execution artifacts exist for any of them.

### §2.3 Scaffolding success modes

**Compressive stacking:** Each layer reduces the theoretical space of the previous, not expands it. Layer A compresses Genesis. Layer B compresses Layer A. And so on. The theoretical space should shrink with each layer, not grow.

**Built closure:** Every spec'd layer has a corresponding execution artifact. No orphans. No "unbuilt" flags.
