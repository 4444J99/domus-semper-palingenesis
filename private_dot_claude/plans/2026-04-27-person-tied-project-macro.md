# Person-Tied Project — Macro Template

> "I want the Maddie, the Rob, and then the suggestion of all that should be the macro of that thing to work elsewhere."

Derived from comparing Maddie (Elevate Align) and Rob (Hokage Chess) thread states at 2026-04-27. The lane structure is universal; specifics fill in per person.

## §1 — The 13 Universal Lanes

Every person-tied project has these lanes. **An empty lane is a vacuum** (per N/A=vacuum rule) — research, fill, or named-defer with reason.

## §2 — Drift signals to watch for

When the trail's lanes diverge from the person's reported state, that's drift. From M+R comparison:

- **Page-count drift** (Maddie: 28 vs 29 vs 45 across artifacts) — when our count and theirs disagree, reconcile *before* shipping more
- **Commit-count drift** (Maddie: 161 vs 202) — same
- **Vocabulary drift** ("formalized tether" vs "agreed in principle") — when the same fact has multiple framings, name which is canonical
- **Cross-lane bridge artifacts** (Drive doc `1YNct507wgoxZR8_e5eqoky_VpPLOlveXTNbj__iAB3I` "the gamified life" appears in BOTH Rob and Maddie clusters) — flag as bridge or misfile, never silently overlap

## §3 — Operating cadence

| Operation | Cadence | Trigger |
|-----------|---------|---------|
| Trail refresh | Weekly OR when ≥3 lanes change | Time-based + change-based |
| Person sync | Per relationship (Rob: 3-cadence warm clock; Maddie: TBD) | Calendar |
| Black-hole follow-up | Per ask, per cadence | L9 + L10 ages out |
| Drift reconciliation | On detection | Person reports state ≠ our record |
| Vacuum filling | On weekly refresh | Empty lane = vacuum, not stable state |

## §4 — Onboarding a new person-tied project (the protocol)

To bring in Scott, Jessica, or future others:

1. **Create `collaborator_<name>.md`** — 1-line description: relationship, brand, last interaction, open threads (high level)
2. **Create `project_artifact_<name>_trail_<date>.md`** — fill all 13 lanes; mark unknowns as VACUUMS to research, not skip
3. **Update MEMORY.md "People" section** with one-liner + link
4. **Identify L3 deliverable + ∇ next concrete action** — without these two, the trail is decorative
5. **Set L2 communication thread + L13 blocked check** — without channel and blockers, the trail can't move
6. **Schedule first refresh in 7 days** — trail without cadence is a snapshot, not a system

## §5 — What stays specific vs what generalizes

The lanes are universal. The contents are specific. **Never force a person into a brand archetype** — Maddie's "sovereignty" and Rob's "Hokage" are real positioning, not template slots. The macro carries:

- Lane structure (universal)
- Cadence pattern (universal)
- Drift detection (universal)
- Vacuum-naming discipline (universal)
- Black-hole closure rule (universal)

The macro does NOT carry:

- Specific lane contents (per-person)
- Brand archetype (per-person, derive from their words)
- Funnel stack choice (per-person; M=GHL, R=Kit, others may differ)
- Aesthetic tokens (per-person)
