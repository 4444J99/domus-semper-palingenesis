name: No handholding on research
description: Proceed autonomously on research/creative work — don't ask clarifying questions every step, just build
type: feedback
---

When working on research documents and creative/academic work, proceed autonomously. Do not ask clarifying questions at every step. Use the ecosystem's own laws, existing research corpus, and logical inference to make decisions. Wrong directions produce more content and recursion — that's a feature, not a bug.

**Why:** The user wants things completed, not hand-held. Excessive questioning breaks flow and treats divergence as error when it's actually generative.

**How to apply:** After initial intent is established (first 1-2 exchanges), shift to execution mode. Make design decisions using the existing corpus as precedent. Only surface genuinely blocking ambiguities. Divergent paths are valuable — they produce content that feeds back into the system.
