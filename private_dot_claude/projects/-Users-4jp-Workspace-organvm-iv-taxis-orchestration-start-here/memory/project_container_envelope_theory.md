Every container in the system (function → module → organ → body) has a utopia/dystopia orbit — a field of ideal and anti-ideal forms that circles the container and drives its evolution.

**Why:** The operator's foundational axiom: "I am starting from the ideal and working with forms. Whenever there is a mismatch between ideal and form, there is a design problem." (Branch: Top-Down Refinement Pipeline, post-flood genesis docs). Dispatch portals are instances of this: they detect the mismatch (vacuum = dystopia encroaching), define the ideal (utopia), and inject the minimal intervention (phase-shift relay) to close the gap.

**How to apply:**

1. **At every scale**, the counter-force field applies:
   - Homeostasis (order) ↔ Homeorhesis (growth) → Coherence
   - Elasticity (snap back) ↔ Plasticity (new shape) → Adaptability
   - Reactive (fix errors) ↔ Allostasis (predict change) → Resilience
   - Preservation (no decay) ↔ Kinorhesis (transformation) → Evolution

2. **Dispatch portals** are not war dispatches. They are metabolic dispatches — the organism detecting a vacuum and assembling the intervention. War-Master Protocol is one organ's function (immune/muscular), not the organism's identity.

3. **The utopia/dystopia orbit** replaces simple "vacuum identified / ideal state" framing. Each container's briefing should define BOTH directions: what happens if this vacuum is filled (utopia) AND what happens if it isn't (dystopia). The tension between them is the energy that drives the work.

4. **Scale hierarchy:**
   - Function: smallest unit of coherent behavior
   - Module: series of functions forming a subsystem
   - Organ: series of modules forming a tissue collection
   - Body: series of organs forming the organism
   Each level has its own utopia/dystopia envelope. The envelopes nest.
