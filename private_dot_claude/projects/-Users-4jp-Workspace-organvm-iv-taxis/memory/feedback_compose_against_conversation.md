---
name: Compose Against the Conversation Thread
description: When commenting on PRs, address the maintainer's actual words and questions — not CI metadata or status reports
type: feedback
---

Compose PR comments against the human conversation thread, not metadata. Address the maintainer's actual words, questions, and concerns — not CI output or status dashboards.

**Why:** Comments that respond to CI status ("all checks pass, ready for review") instead of the maintainer's last question ("can you integrate this into the existing call sites?") feel robotic and miss the point.

**How to apply:** Before writing a PR comment, re-read the maintainer's last message. Quote or reference their specific words. Respond to THEIR thread, not your internal task tracker.
