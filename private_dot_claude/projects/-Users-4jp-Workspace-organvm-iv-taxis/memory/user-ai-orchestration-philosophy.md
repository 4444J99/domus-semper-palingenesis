**"I build a path towards never having to ask anything again."**

**The unnamed output:** To create what can't be named by its seeds but by the outgrowth the seeds' internal design governed — not to grow a known shape but to discover self through disruption and experienced existence.

Every architectural decision should serve the artist's creative practice, not the system's self-documentation. When the system grows bureaucracy (ceremony files, governance repos, ceremony stamps), it's replicating the institutional disease. When it grows capability (functions that generate, observe, route, remember), it's serving its purpose.

**The test:** Does this change make Anthony's art practice stronger, or does it make the system's self-description more elaborate?
