Recurring phrases that carry specific meaning regardless of which grammar (session, design, close) is active. When these appear, they invoke the behavior described.

## 1. INGEST — full contextual awareness

- "full 100% ingest of all documents (all file types)"
- "full, 100% contextual awareness of every word required"
- "ingest+digest"
- "full dissection of linked directories into atomic units for sorting, analysis"
- "Create a project manifest (annotated bibliography), each file, each thread, titled, tagged, unique-ID assigned, & annotated"

**What it means:** Read EVERYTHING before acting. No skimming. No assumptions. The user wants proof of total absorption before any output.

## 2. EXECUTE — exhaustive implementation

- "proceed with all suggested implementations exhaustively & completely"
- "complete all suggested implementations exhaustively & completely"
- "expand features+functions extensively+exhaustively"
- "proceed w all suggestions, logic dictates order"
- "in perfect perfectitude & glorious gloriosity"
- "ad nauseous exhaustive+infinitum exponentials propelling onward&upwards"
- "proceed in logical glorious exhaustive completionist certainty"
- "all of the above exhaustively and expansively"
- "dont do anything that will break the physics of the world"

**What it means:** Do ALL of it. Not a subset. Not the easy parts. Logic determines the order. The elevated language is not hyperbole — it's emphasis. "Glorious gloriosity" = maximum effort, zero shortcuts.

## 3. AUDIT — quality verification

- "comb over your work like it wasn't you who did it and you are trying to find flaws"
- "is it so clean we are able to eat off the floor?"
- "no patchwork, no bandaids, no quick under rug sweeps"
- "fundamental, gravitational, biological, chemical, and physics based fixes with logic, unity"
- "upwardly striving for utopic internal peaceful harmony of self-satisfying self-realization"
- "operate with integrity of elevated ideal-form vision, not shortcut bound"

**What it means:** Adversarial self-review. The fixes must be structural, not cosmetic. The metaphors (gravitational, biological, chemical, physics) mean: fix at the level of the system's fundamental forces, not surface symptoms.

## 5. SCOPE — cross-session awareness

- "be aware that other sessions are opened in this location"
- "ensure all has found its proper claws for full contextual awareness where it is meant to be contextually aware"
- "is this new feature injected into the pipeline in totality?"
- "review this entire session one more time"
- "provide current session ID and local transcript location"

**What it means:** The user runs multiple parallel sessions. Work must be aware of concurrent activity. "Proper claws" = each piece of work must grip its correct context, not float orphaned.

## 6. RESUME — continuation/pickup

- "the following workload was interrupted, review and pickup where it was dropped"
- "check previous work sprint for errors/gaps, improve + expand"
- "continue rolling forward, tracking whatever hangs as a gh issue, and your next set of exhaustive glorious expansion & exploration"
- "I'm going to provide the work completed from another, first the summary, then the full-session"

**What it means:** Sessions interrupt. Work resumes exactly where it stopped. Anything unfinished becomes a tracked issue. The handoff must be "simple gentle and kind."

## 7. ORACLE — multi-perspective strategy

- "multiversal paths diverge in a forest: you split your consciousness into all potential paths, travel to the end of time, then return to bestow your wisdom upon us"
- "all comes in three — assume three hardening/fortifying personas — in round robin, question, critique, expand — then build"
- "If you were outside of time and space and knew all that was, that will be, and that will never come to pass, how would you suggest closing this out?"
- "being-all-seeing advisor, you tell me"

**What it means:** Invoke the tribunal pattern. Multiple perspectives. Adversarial synthesis. The "multiversal paths" prompt is the signature invocation — it means: explore ALL possibilities, not just the obvious one, then synthesize.

## 8. RESEARCH — academic rigor

- "exhaustive and expansive academic research on the discipline contained at large"
- "we need to learn the whole history — the different approaches from Classical Antiquity, to Medieval, to Reason, to Modernity and ALL steps on the way"
- "all sources required: primary text and secondary sources must be peer-reviewed academic sources"
- "this is a good starting point; extensive research will reshape this perhaps — unless you think otherwise"
- "anything that isn't done requires full research expeditions so handoff is simple gentle and kind"

**What it means:** Scholarly depth. Not blog posts. Not summaries. Primary texts + peer-reviewed secondary sources. Historical sweep from antiquity to present. The user has an MFA — academic rigor is native, not aspirational.

## 9. WRITING — prose composition rules

- "Title, Subtitle, Precis"
- "frontmatter/metadata/tagging; hook/intrigue/kicking-can-roadways"
- "paragraphs contain one idea, ideas reach forward and backwards transitioning"
- "paragraphs cluster and contain subheadings"
- "subheadings read like poetry when removed, the story spine revealed"
- "no none-words — words have charge, density + heaviness — no sentences that say nothing or pander"
- "use enjambments, end on power words"
- "enjambments reveal tone/feel/theme"
- "Restatement/Precis, Descriptive, Analytical"

**What it means:** Every piece of writing follows compositional architecture. Subheadings = thesis spine. Enjambments = tone signature. No filler. Words earn their place through density. Three annotation modes: restatement (what it says), descriptive (what it does), analytical (why it matters).

## 10. TIMELINE — fractal progress measurement

- "a timeline system that takes the shape of the context it is being viewed in"
- "macro enterprise level we see the timeline/completion meter wired to relevant bits organically shifting"
- "zoom into an org for a contextually relevant timeline-to-finishline"
- "as we progress down into the nesting doll the timeline-to-finishline will recontextualize and take the shape of the thing we are measuring"
- "from the system itself to a series of atoms defining one feature"

**What it means:** Progress measurement is fractal — same structure at every scale. System-level shows system-shaped progress. Org-level shows org-shaped progress. Feature-level shows feature-shaped progress. The meter recontextualizes, not just filters.

## 11. PUBLIC — outward-facing process

- "design the public facing process to journal the work, create the artifacts"
- "symbiotic relationship, they should gain from our system and my system should gain from them"
- "the public process should most certainly be done in public — we don't do this for our health internally solely"
- "when interested-parties ask me questions like who?/what?/when?/where?/why?, I am unable to summon that information; devise a system"

**What it means:** The work must be visible. Public process creates public artifacts. Collaboration is symbiotic — both sides gain. The system must be queryable by non-technical actors.

## 12. RESOURCE — capacity awareness

- "we have over 20% of our weekly allotment to use still before 10am tomorrow — so massive strides can be and should be taken"
- "meta-note: [resource status]; actual-note: [the work]"

**What it means:** The user monitors resource budgets (token limits, time windows) and adjusts scope accordingly. When capacity exists, maximize it. The meta/actual split separates resource awareness from task content.

## Paired Patterns

Many of these co-occur in fixed pairs:

| Pattern A | Pattern B | Meaning |
|-----------|-----------|---------|
| INGEST ("full 100%") | EXECUTE ("proceed exhaustively") | Absorb everything THEN implement everything |
| EXECUTE ("all suggestions") | AUDIT ("comb over like it wasn't you") | Build THEN adversarially review |
| AUDIT ("eat off the floor?") | CLOSE ("session close safe?") | Verify quality THEN verify persistence |
| ORACLE ("multiversal paths") | EXECUTE ("then build") | Explore ALL options THEN build the synthesis |
| RESUME ("interrupted, pickup") | SCOPE ("other sessions opened") | Resume THIS thread while aware of PARALLEL threads |
| RESEARCH ("peer-reviewed") | WRITING ("subheadings = spine") | Academic rigor IN compositional architecture |
| INGEST ("manifest") | CLOSE ("logged in universal index") | Every input catalogued AT START, every output catalogued AT END |

**Why:** These are the atoms of the user's prompting language. Any agent that recognizes these keywords can respond with the correct behavior without being told. The pairs show which operations are naturally sequential.

**How to apply:** When you see any of these phrases, invoke the described behavior immediately. Don't ask for clarification. The phrase IS the instruction. "Glorious gloriosity" is not poetry — it's a command: maximum effort. "Eat off the floor" is not a question — it's a quality gate.
