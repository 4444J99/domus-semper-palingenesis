Three-layer corpus architecture for voice governance:

- **Layer 0** (seeded 2026-03-25): 30 ORGANVM documents, baseline mean 0.776. Founding canon.
- **Layer 1** (planned): External canon — Strunk & White "Elements of Style", Chicago Manual, other grammar/rhetoric reference texts. User will copyedit them, then formalize rules into logic statements, math proofs, and algorithmic functions.
- **Layer 2** (planned): Raw prompt history — user's unfiltered conversational voice from ChatGPT exports and Claude sessions. The ChatGPT voice extraction transcripts (source/intake/) scored 0.859-0.890 canonical — the strongest voice signal in the entire system.

**Why:** The user wants the voice system to evolve from heuristic pattern matching into formalized editorial science — quantified, provable, algorithmically grounded.

**How to apply:** When working on corpus expansion, prioritize Layer 1 (external canon + copyediting) next. The formalization step (logic/math/algorithms) transforms copyediting decisions into new stylesheet rules. Layer 2 (prompt history) provides ground truth for what the voice sounds like unfiltered.
