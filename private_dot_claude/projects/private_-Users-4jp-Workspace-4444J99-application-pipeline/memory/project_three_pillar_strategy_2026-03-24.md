description: Strategic pivot from job-only pipeline to three revenue channels — jobs (runway), grants/funding (validation + non-dilutive capital), consulting/retainer (bridge to studio). ORGANVM studio is the north star.
type: project
---

The pipeline needs to serve three pillars equally, not just jobs:

**Pillar 1: Employment (Jobs)** — runway. Temporary funding mechanism. Ideal job feeds the studio (remote, ORGANVM-aligned work). Currently 90% of pipeline effort.

**Pillar 2: Grants & Funding** — validation + non-dilutive capital. Creative Capital (opens 2026-04-02), Prix Ars, S+T+ARTS ecosystem. Positions ORGANVM as recognized artistic practice. Currently secondary track.

**Pillar 3: Consulting/Retainer** — the bridge to the studio. Tony Carbone (MCA + Flyland.com), Scott Lefler (build+sell partner). Recurring revenue, relationship-based, full autonomy. Currently 2 entries, no dedicated workflow.

**North star:** Build ORGANVM into a studio — a Pixar, an ILM. The job is venture capital paid with time. Grants fund the art. Consulting builds the client base. The studio already exists structurally (113 repos, 8 organs). It needs revenue.

**Why:** User explicitly stated: "my dream is to build this system up into a studio, a Pixar, an ILM." Working for others is challenging, wants freedom, wants to travel. Job is "the thing I want least but kind of need most."

**Pipeline changes needed:**
- Scoring rubric needs pillar-specific dimensions
- "Max 10 active entries" may need per-pillar caps
- Identity position 9 (Founder/Operator) needs outreach templates, scoring hooks, dedicated workflow
- Time allocation may need consulting-specific block
- Track field needs "consulting" as first-class citizen with its own lifecycle

**How to apply:** When making pipeline decisions, always evaluate against all three pillars. Don't over-invest in jobs at the expense of grants and consulting. The studio dream is the filter — does this action move toward studio viability?

**Data context (2026-03-24):**
- Era 1: 2,000 cold apps → 0 interviews (2024-2025, 10 months)
- Era 2: 20 structured apps, no outreach → 0 interviews
- Era 3: 60 submissions + 271 outreach + 30 DMs → 0 interviews BUT 6 responses (20% DM response rate), 8 days old
- 10/23 submitted entries have warm paths (43%), 6 cold are grants/writing (different cycle)
