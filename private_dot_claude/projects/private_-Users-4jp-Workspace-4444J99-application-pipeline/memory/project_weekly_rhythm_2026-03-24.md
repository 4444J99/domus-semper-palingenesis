**Creation is the constant. Everything else orbits it.**

The user's words: "the closest alignment with the universe at large is creation — god created, we are all our own god — I yearn to create." Building happens every day. Artifact posting daily. The three revenue pillars exist to fund and amplify creation, not to replace it.

**Weekly rhythm (creation-first):**

Daily constant (every day):
- Build. Create. Ship artifacts. Post to LinkedIn/portfolio.
- 15 min pipeline check: responses, DMs, `run.py morning`

Monday — Pillar 1 (Jobs): Batch Day
- Review week's sourced jobs, score top 5-10, apply to best 3-5
- Protocol-validated DMs to linked contacts
- ONLY day for job applications

Tuesday — Pillar 2 (Grants & Funding)
- Research open windows, draft materials, advance qualified entries
- 1 grant/residency submission per week minimum
- Before April 2: Creative Capital prep takes priority

Wednesday — Pillar 3 (Consulting)
- Tony/Scott follow-up, proposals, scope documents
- Consulting work IS studio work — building for clients feeds the studio

Thursday — Deep build. No outreach.
Friday — Light pipeline hygiene + build.

**Key constraints:**
- Monday is the ONLY day for job submissions (governor against 2,000-app instinct)
- LinkedIn connection ceiling is a feature, not a bug — forces precision
- Daily artifact posting compounds visibility and attracts inbound
- The 20% DM response rate > 0% from 2,000 cold apps

**Why:** User has a "bang head against wall" instinct with volume. The weekly structure channels that energy. The creation-first framing ensures the studio IS the work, not something deferred until the pillars produce.

**How to apply:** When the user asks "what should I do today?" — creation first, pillar second. When tempted to batch-apply to 20 jobs on a Tuesday, redirect to the grant work. The rhythm is the discipline.
