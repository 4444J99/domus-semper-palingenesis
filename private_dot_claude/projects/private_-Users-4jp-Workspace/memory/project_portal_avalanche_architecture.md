Portals are reality-rectification interventions that open where rendered reality and imagined ideal diverge. Two futures (utopic/dystopic) orbit every container; the portal injects form to shift the trajectory.

**Why:** The operator manages parallel sessions across multiple agents (Claude, Gemini, Codex, Copilot). Each portal is a self-contained intervention that deposits its forward state for the next portal. No awkward walk-in (BRIEFING is the war report). No awkward exit (RECEIPT.md IS the baton).

**The 5-file genome:**
- `CLAUDE.md` — Portal nature + counter-force field + lifecycle
- `BRIEFING.md` — War report: front line, utopic/dystopic attractors, deliverables
- `REFERENCES.md` — Keys to doors + context stream + terrain
- `SEQUENCE.md` — Intervention procedure α→ε: inject, create, operate, verify, precipitate
- `RECEIPT.md` — Forward deposit (written at completion, consumed by next portal)

**Lifecycle:** `INJECT → CREATE → OPERATE → VERIFY → PRECIPITATE → STOP`
- No EVAPORATE phase. Evaporation is the next portal's prerogative.
- PRECIPITATE writes RECEIPT.md + optionally seeds next portal's BRIEFING.md.
- STOP is absolute. The operator reads the receipt and decides.

**Scaffolder:** `scripts/scaffold-portal.py` — generates complete genome from slug + archetype.

**First instance:** `tasks/communications-correspondence-relay/` in orchestration-start-here. All 5 files present. RECEIPT.md written during S52 hardening pass.

**How to apply:** When the operator describes a mismatch between what is and what should be, open a portal. When you see a `tasks/` directory, it contains active interventions. When you see a `RECEIPT.md`, the previous portal completed.
