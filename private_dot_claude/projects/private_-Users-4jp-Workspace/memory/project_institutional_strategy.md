ORGANVM's legal/financial institutionalization strategy was researched and designed on 2026-03-24.

**Why:** Aspiration Tech — mission-aligned with tech/data, full back-office + strategic mentoring, sliding 5-15% fees, supports spin-out. Contact: fiscalservices@aspirationtech.org ($150 app fee).

**How to apply:** Organ mapping — Organs I, II, IV (open-source), VI, META go through nonprofit/sponsored project. Organs III, V, VII go through LLC. Open-core IP model.

- Phase 4 (year 3+): Full Mozilla model (Foundation owns PBC)
