Do not recommend phone calls for pricing or negotiation conversations. User communicates with precision in text and gets steamrolled verbally.

**Why:** User explicitly said "I dont like to talk over the phone--i am weak and susceptible to overpowering." Maddie is enthusiastic and fast-talking — in a phone call, the pricing conversation would drift without landing on numbers.

**How to apply:** Draft pricing as a structured text message, not talking points for a call. Keep it short, one-read, with a specific number. Match the professional tone of the documents already being sent (roadmap, design decisions). The user controls tempo in text — that's the advantage.
